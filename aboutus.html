About Us

A website for hiring and finding a job

most of unemployed people waste their time looking for a job on a board and filling unnecessary forms to apply including making a bunch of copies of their CV .So to minimize this tiresome process and to make job opportunities available for all people, we build this website .People can see available jobs with the necessary information and can apply using this platform.

Goal of AfriHire

we offer an opportunity for the people to have an easy acess for a job and make a deal with the company remotely.After five years we have a plan to work with 1000 companies and 40,000 employees .
